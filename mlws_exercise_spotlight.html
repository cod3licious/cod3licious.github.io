Exercise: Industry Spotlights

  • What kind of problem the company is solving
  • How the company makes or saves money with this (from the perspective of this company, e.g., if they sell a Software-as-a-Service solution that optimizes the internal processes of their clients, then the company makes money by selling the SaaS)

  • What category of ML algorithm(s) they are using in their solution
