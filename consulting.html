consulting

My mission is to empower organizations to develop their own data science solutions, ensuring long-term success through comprehensive training, support, and cutting-edge expertise. I'm committed to fostering self-reliance and driving sustainable growth by enabling businesses to harness the full potential of data science in their operations and decision-making processes.

Whether you are new to the world of data science or seeking to enhance your existing capabilities, I'm here to guide you through the process. Have a look at the different consulting packages I offer - and if you had something else in mind, feel free to contact me anyways!

Discover the Possibilities

Understand the potential of machine learning (ML) for your organization and get started with your data science journey:

  • Get an overview of how ML works and explore the opportunities it offers
  • Together, we'll identify project ideas with the highest potential impact, while ensuring their feasibility within your unique business context
  • Have a clear roadmap for your first ML project and actionable steps to prepare your data infrastructure for success

Develop Data Science Expertise

For long-term success, investing in an in-house data science team is key:

  • We'll assess the specific skills required to achieve your ML goals and determine the right roles for your team (Data Scientist? ML Engineer? What's the difference?)
  • Get support in hiring top talent with compelling job postings and technical candidate assessment
  • Empower existing employees to transition into data science roles through tailored ML training programs

Implement and Succeed

As your trusted coach and sparring partner, I will support your internal data science team throughout the implementation process:

  • Feedback on conceptual design and software architecture of the ML solution
  • Comprehensive code reviews and implementation support (using Python)
  • Together, we'll integrate the ML solution into your workflows and set up monitoring systems for future success
