I'm a freelance data scientist & Python developer with a PhD in machine learning (ML) from the TU Berlin (Germany). Have a look at my CV and feel free to contact me about freelance opportunities!

I would love to support you in your data science projects, show your team how to successfully apply machine learning algorithms, or help you recruit capable data scientists for permanent positions.

"The only constant in life is change."
This insight, first attributed to the Greek philosopher Heraclitus, also applies to your data, which is the foundation of any machine learning solution. To adapt to the inevitable data and concept drifts, it is necessary to continuously monitor and update a machine learning model deployed in production. Therefore, the only sustainable approach to data science consulting includes enabling your employees to maintain and improve machine learning solutions on their own. This is why a structured ML training program and individual coaching sessions are the cornerstone of my services.

Furthermore, I believe that contributing to open source & open knowledge benefits everyone, which is why I've published a free online book describing machine learning best practices & how to avoid common pitfalls.
