ML for Executives Workshop

This course is aimed at executives, who have an interest in machine learning.

It covers very similar topics as the ML for Data Strategists workshop, only without the exercises:

  • "Introduction"
  • "Data Analysis & Preprocessing"
  • "Supervised Learning" (the section on "Deep Learning (Neural Networks)" is optional)
  • "Avoiding Common Pitfalls" (the sections on "Pitfall #1" and "Pitfall #2" are optional)
  • Watch the Video: "Data Science in Practice" (which includes a short recap of the theory; 33 min - optional)
  • "Conclusion"
